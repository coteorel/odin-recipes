Orange Zest Chicken Recipe

Description

Experience a burst of citrus flavor with Orange Zest Chicken. Marinate tender chicken pieces in a zesty mixture of orange zest, garlic, and fresh herbs.

Grill or roast until the chicken is juicy and infused with the delightful essence of oranges. This recipe is a refreshing twist on traditional chicken dishes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Orange zest
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary or thyme)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine orange zest, minced garlic, chopped fresh herbs, olive oil, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  2. Place the chicken pieces in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.
  4. Grill or roast the marinated chicken until it's cooked through and has a lovely citrus aroma, typically 15-20 minutes on the grill or 25-30 minutes in the oven.
  5. Serve your Orange Zest Chicken with your favorite sides and savor the vibrant flavors. Enjoy!
